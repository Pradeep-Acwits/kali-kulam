FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your demo. Still have questions? Email us.

What types of assessments does Discover Assessments offer? +
We offer a comprehensive suite of psychometric assessments including cognitive ability tests, personality and behavioral profiling, situational judgment tests, emotional intelligence assessments, 360° multi-rater feedback, leadership potential evaluations, and custom-built assessments. All are available in gamified, game-themed, and standard formats.
How long does it take to implement the platform? +
Most clients are up and running within 2–5 business days for standard assessments. For custom-built assessments, the timeline is typically 4–8 weeks including design, validation, and norm-setting. There is no complex technical integration required — our platform is entirely cloud-based.
Are the assessments scientifically validated? +
Yes. All our assessments are developed and validated by qualified I/O psychologists following rigorous psychometric standards for reliability, validity, and fairness. We maintain global and regional norm databases across industries and geographies to ensure accurate benchmarking.
Can assessments be used across multiple countries and languages? +
Absolutely. We support 15+ languages and are currently deployed in 40+ countries. Our assessments are culturally adapted — not just translated — to ensure fairness and accuracy for diverse candidate populations. We have regional norm groups for the Middle East, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Europe, and Africa.
What is gamified psychometric assessment? +
Gamified assessments use game mechanics — narratives, characters, interactive scenarios, and reward systems — to make psychometric testing more engaging. Our Astro-themed platform is the world's first to offer fully game-based, game-themed, and gamified assessments. This increases candidate completion rates to over 90% and yields more authentic, accurate data.
Is the platform GDPR compliant? +
Yes. Discover Assessments is fully GDPR compliant and follows international data privacy standards. Candidate data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. We provide full data processing agreements (DPAs) and support your compliance obligations as a data controller.
